I design and build intuitive digital products by bridging user experience, product thinking, and engineering. My work focuses on creating meaningful interactions that balance aesthetics, usability, and real-world business impact.

01 - Background

Background

I’m a Computer Science graduate turned UX Specialist and Product Designer passionate about creating intuitive, human-centered digital experiences. Frustrating and poorly designed products sparked my curiosity to solve real-world problems through design. I work at the intersection of product design and engineering, combining user research, visual design, and front-end development to craft cohesive products and meaningful user experiences.

Collaboration

I collaborate closely with product managers, designers, engineers, marketing and stakeholders to translate ideas into clear product strategies and intuitive user experiences.

With a foundation in front-end development, I bridge the gap between design and implementation to craft seamless, user-centered products.

Impact & Volunteering

I believe good user experience is a fundamental human right, which inspires my volunteer work with the Pistis Foundation. Through the foundation’s Ubomi Medical Outreach Program, 48,102 medical interventions have been delivered to underserved communities as of 2025.

In 2024, my work in the Communications department earned me recognition as one of the foundation’s best-performing volunteers, reflecting my commitment to improving workflows and championing accessibility.

Beyond Work

Beyond pixels and code, I explore cultures and travel widely. Representing Cotrust Equity in Tunis, Tunisia to highlight initiatives expanding access to finance for Africans was a memorable experience that continues to inspire my work.

I remain committed to empowering people and crafting meaningful, human-centered experiences.
