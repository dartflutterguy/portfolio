Real-Time Creative Performance Platform

SWINGSELL

Designed and implemented a scalable A/B testing system enabling campaign managers to create, monitor, and optimize experiments with real-time insights.

Role

  • Product & Interaction Design
  • Research & Insight Analysis
  • Information Architecture

Team

  • Designer (Me)
  • Engineering
  • Marketing

Industry

  • Digital Advertising
  • Adtech

Timeline

  • 3 Months
01 - THE CHALLENGE
  • Campaign managers experienced long and inefficient testing cycles.
  • Experiment creation and monitoring were fragmented across multiple workflows.
  • Limited real-time visibility delayed performance-based decisions.
  • Manual configurations increased setup friction and risk of errors.
  • Slower optimization cycles impacted campaign effectiveness.

The objective was to design a scalable A/B testing system that centralized experiment management, reduced workflow friction, and enabled faster, data-driven campaign optimization.

02 - RESEARCH & KEY INSIGHTS

Research Activities

  • Conducted qualitative interviews with active platform users.
  • Reviewed quantitative usage data to identify workflow bottlenecks.
  • Analyzed existing test creation and monitoring processes.

Key Insights

  • Users needed clearer visibility into active and completed experiments.
  • Experiment setup required too many configuration steps.
  • Lack of centralized tracking created confusion.
  • Timely feedback significantly influenced optimization decisions.
03 - DESIGN PROCESS & IDEATION

Methodology

Guided by lean design thinking principles, I moved from problem framing to rapid prototyping and validation, ensuring the final solution balanced usability, system constraints, and real campaign workflows.

Information Architecture

I structured the system to centralize experiment management by organizing active tests, historical data, and performance metrics into a unified dashboard, reducing cognitive load and enabling users to make faster decisions.

Wireframing & Workflow Structuring

I translated research insights into low-fidelity wireframes, mapping out the sequential flow for test creation and the dashboard layout. This iterative process helped validate assumptions before moving to high-fidelity designs.

04 - THE FINAL SOLUTION

The final solution introduced a centralized experimentation system that streamlined test creation, improved experiment visibility, and enabled real-time performance monitoring.

Unified A/B Test Dashboard

The dashboard provides users with immediate visibility into all active and completed experiments, reducing navigation friction.

  • Centralized visibility of active and completed tests.
  • Clear status indicators for monitoring experiment progress.
  • Reduced navigation friction through structured layout.
  • Improved overall cognitive clarity.

Navigation for A/B Testing

A centralized workspace where users can access and manage experiments across all stages including ongoing, completed, and draft tests.

  • Clear separation of test states for better visibility.
  • Reduced friction when locating and resuming active experiments
  • Immediate clarity on experiment status at a glance.

Streamlined Test Creation Workflow

Instead of scattered configuration forms, the workflow guides users through a sequential process, reducing errors and setup time.

  • Converted complex multi-step configuration into a guided sequential flow.
  • Structured input stages reduced setup errors.
  • Improved efficiency for campaign managers.
  • Enabled faster experiment launch.

Real-Time Analytics & Management

A live performance layer that enables campaign managers to monitor experiment metrics and manage creatives directly from a unified interface.

  • Real-time visibility into key performance metrics.
  • Ability to adjust and manage creatives without leaving the workspace.
  • Faster optimization decisions driven by live data.

Notification Center (Post-Beta Iteration)

After beta testing revealed awareness gaps, we introduced a notification center that alerts users to critical experiment updates in real-time.

  • Introduced after beta feedback revealed awareness gaps.
  • Delivered real-time alerts on experiment updates.
  • Improved user responsiveness and decision confidence.
05 - VALIDATION & PRODUCTION LAUNCH
  • Beta tested with 25 active users.
  • Collected structured feedback across usability and workflow clarity.
  • Identified need for enhanced experiment visibility.
  • Implemented notification center based on findings.
  • Successfully released to production.
  • Observed increased adoption following launch.
06 - OUTCOMES & LEARNINGS

Outcomes

  • Shipped a production-ready experimentation system.
  • Increased feature adoption post-release.
  • Improved workflow efficiency for campaign managers.
  • Centralized experiment visibility across the platform.
  • Enabled faster campaign optimization cycles.

Key Learnings

  • Clarity and structure are critical in experimentation tools.
  • Real-time visibility directly influences user confidence.
  • Iterative testing significantly enhances perceived usability.
  • Simplified workflows drive stronger adoption.
