A Unified Web & Mobile Experience

COTRUST

How a redesigned website and a new mobile app digitized cluster-based lending and expanded access to finance for traders across Africa.

01 - CONTEXT & CHALLENGE

CoTrust was expanding its financial services platform to support micro and small traders across Africa. However, many core activities within the ecosystem were still handled through manual processes and paperwork, creating friction for traders and cluster managers.

As the platform scaled, CoTrust needed a digital experience that could support growing demand while simplifying access to financial services.

The project involved two parallel efforts:

  • Redesigning the public website to clearly communicate CoTrust’s platform and services.
  • Designing the mobile application from scratch, enabling traders to manage financial activities digitally.

The challenge was translating complex, community-based financial workflows into a scalable digital product.

02 - RESEARCH & PRODUCT DIRECTION

Early discovery revealed that traders relied heavily on physical documentation and manual coordination within clusters to access loans and track repayments.

Several insights shaped the product direction:

  • Traders needed a central place to manage financial activities instead of relying on paperwork.
  • Loan requests and repayments required clear tracking to build trust in the system.
  • Cluster champions needed oversight tools without complicating the experience for individual traders.

A key challenge was designing a system that supported two interconnected roles:

  • Individual traders accessing financial services
  • Cluster champions managing trader groups

The information architecture had to support both roles while keeping the product simple and accessible.

03 - SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE CHALLENGE

One of the most complex product decisions involved designing a unified system for both traders and cluster champions.

While traders required simple workflows for requesting loans and tracking repayments, champions needed broader visibility into group activity and credit performance.

The architecture was structured to support these different responsibilities without fragmenting the overall user experience.

04 - MOBILE PRODUCT EXPERIENCE

The mobile application became the primary platform for traders to manage their financial activities.

Users could:

  • Request loans directly through the app
  • Track disbursement and repayment progress
  • Join clusters and view community updates
  • Monitor their financial activity in one place

The experience was designed to simplify financial processes that were previously handled offline, while also supporting cluster champions in overseeing trader groups.

05 - WEB PLATFORM EXPERIENCE

The website redesign focused on clearly explaining CoTrust’s platform and financial model to users, partners, and investors.

The new experience improved how visitors understood the cluster-based lending system and the value CoTrust provided to traders.

  • Clarified the platform’s product offering
  • Strengthened product marketing and storytelling
  • Built credibility with users, partners, and investors

Structured Navigation & Information Hierarchy

A multi-level navigation system that organizes Cotrust’s products, services, and other resources into clear categories, enabling users to quickly discover relevant information without overwhelming the interface.

Interactive FAQ for Financial Clarity

An expandable FAQ section designed to help users quickly understand Cotrust’s lending model, repayment structure, and eligibility requirements. Motion and progressive disclosure reduce cognitive load while guiding users through complex financial information.

06 - SUPPORTING FINANCIAL WORKFLOWS

The platform introduced digital workflows that simplified how traders interacted with financial services.

Key capabilities included:

  • Submitting loan requests through the mobile app
  • Tracking loan disbursement and repayments
  • Monitoring cluster participation and updates
  • Supporting champions in managing trader groups

Cluster champions helped maintain healthy networks by monitoring repayments and ensuring strong credit performance within their clusters.

07 - DEPLOYMENT & MARKETPLACE ASSETS

As part of the product launch, visual assets were designed for publishing the application on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

These assets ensured the product was clearly presented across marketplace listings and supported the platform’s transition from offline operations to a digital experience.

08 - PRODUCT IMPACT

The redesigned platform helped CoTrust successfully transition key financial services from manual processes to a digital system.

  • Traders could request and manage loans directly through the mobile app
  • Onboarding into clusters became faster and more accessible
  • Users gained clearer visibility into financial activity and repayments
  • The platform strengthened trust between traders, champions, and investors

By combining a clear web presence with a functional mobile product, CoTrust expanded its ability to support traders and improve financial inclusion across its network.
